## What to do with this report

The **MISSING_IN_CANON** and **EMPTY** buckets are the shopping list. For each narrator, fill the canon fields the eval actually references (not the whole template tree). Fields you genuinely don't know — lost ancestor dates, etc. — can stay empty; the scorer policy should distinguish "canon says X" from "canon silent."

Schema-mismatch rows (non-`—` in the `resolved` column) indicate fieldPath aliases the extractor/eval use that don't match the template key structure (e.g., `family.spouse.*` → `spouse.*`). Those are eval-harness issues, not canon gaps, and should be fixed in the eval-v2 rebuild WO.
